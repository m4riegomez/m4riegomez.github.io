Presentation on an Overview of the Utilities Sector

2023

I made this presentation for a project in my finance course, Financial Institutions & Markets.

I provided an overview of the industries within the Utilities sector in the S&P 500 as classified by the GICS. By researching IBIS World and Business Insights, I gathered key observations and current data trends relevant to the sector and its industries. I concluded with a fundamental analysis of the intermediate-to-long term economic outlook of the sector and its industries.

The class also viewed a ten-minute video presentation I provided on my analysis.
